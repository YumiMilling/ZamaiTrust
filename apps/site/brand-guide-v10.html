Brand Colours
Built around Evergreen.

The system has one centre of gravity: Evergreen. Everything else exists in relation to it. Copper provides the warmth Evergreen withholds. Surfaces are warm-dark — a barely perceptible warm undertone creates temperature contrast against Evergreen's cool teal. When Evergreen lands, it commands full attention.

EG
Evergreen
#073233 — Foundation colour

Deep, still, rooted. The colour of the Zambian forest before dawn. The foundation — not a background tint but a deliberate surface. Appears in the footer, in hero moments, as borders and active indicators. When used, it is a statement. When absent, it is anticipated.

#A86B2A — Accent colour

Warm, mineral, hard-earned. Zambia sits on the Copperbelt — this colour is a birthright. It provides the warmth Evergreen withholds: eyebrow labels, dividers, the single stripe of heat in an otherwise cool composition.
